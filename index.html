NEED HELP?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, products, and beauty consultations.

How do I book a beauty consultation? +

You can book a consultation through our website by selecting your preferred beauty expert and choosing an available time slot.

Are virtual consultations available? +

Yes, we offer both virtual and in-person consultations to accommodate your preferences and schedule.

What's the difference between skincare consultation and makeup artistry? +

Skincare consultation focuses on your skin health and routine, while makeup artistry covers makeup techniques and application.

Do I need a subscription to access services? +

No subscription required! You can book individual consultations or purchase service packages based on your needs.

Can I switch beauty experts if I don't feel the right connection? +

We want you to feel comfortable with your beauty expert. You can easily switch to another professional at any time.

Is my information and consultation history kept confidential? +

Yes, all your personal information and consultation details are kept strictly confidential and secure.
